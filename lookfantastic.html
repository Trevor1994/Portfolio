LookFantastic Project

The following project was proposed by the HutGroup, who are a global leader in health, beuty, fitness and lifestyle. The aim of this project was to rebuild the checkout payment form on LookFantastic to make it easier for customers to enter the relevant information across the different devices.

The first part of the process was looking at the LookFantastic checkout form as well as another one, which was Topman. This helped gain a better understanding of what the requirements were and what features were required.

I used the mobile-first approach to build the application.
