WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT STUDENT

Final Year Project

In a classroom scenario, the lecturer will frequently want to ask questions of their class, get the students responses, and then evaluate and display the results. Thus, why the goal of this project is to develop a web-based solution that allows lecturers to present a set of questions, students to answer these, and the results to be graphed in real-time. The system is aimed at both schools and higher educations such as Edge Hill University.
